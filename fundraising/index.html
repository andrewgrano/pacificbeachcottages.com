Fundraising

We all hear of people or groups in the community facing difficult times and desperately trying to raise needed funds. Unfortunately as much as we would like, we realize we are unable to give to every cause.

We have developed a unique way to help non-profit organizations and causes raise those needed funds.

The cottages are perfect prizes for raffle and silent auction fundraising.

How Does it Work? It's easy:

  • Your group sells raffle tickets at $10 and up (you decide).
  • The winner receives 2 NIGHTS STAY* for 2 at the Cottage
  • *Winner pays cleaning fee
  • Group agrees to retain a $200.00 security deposit to be used ONLY in the event of damage to the Cottage caused by Winner
Fundraising possibilities are endless
