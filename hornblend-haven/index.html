This beautiful 1 bed 1 bath cottage is located just 5 blocks to the beach. Queen bed in bedroom and queen sofa bed in living room. Kitchen is fully equipped and includes: ice maker fridge, washer/dryer. Bathroom includes shower and basic toiletries. Tons of amenities: outdoor shower, bikes, boogie boards, beach chairs/towels, pack'n'play, bike trailer, BBQ, firepit and so much more. Garage parking included.
