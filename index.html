About the Neighborhood

    • Discover the ‘Best of Pacific Beach.’ Garnet Avenue has everything you could ask for: restaurants, bars, shops, hair/nail salons, grocery stores, you name it, Garnet Avenue has it all! Head west from your cottage and voila! ‘Door to Shore’ in minutes. You are at the beach. Enjoy!

    And if that isn’t enough, take an 11 min ride to the rollercoaster at Belmont Park, a 17 min ride and enjoy Mission Bay, hang with ocean mammals after just a 9 min drive to SeaWorld, or 12 mins takes you to the world class San Diego Zoo and in just 38 mins you could be in Tijuana, Mexico (remember your passport).
